How to take care

All cacti are known for their drought tolerance and are easy to care for.

Water

The factor that is quite important to cactus players, often making the mistake of causing dead plants is watering. Mammillaria Plumosa is usually placed in two environments: office, indoors and outdoors. If you keep it in the house and office, it should be watered once every 2 weeks if the environment is not too dry. As for trees for outdoor bancol, it should be covered and watered once a week. Note that each watering time also only needs to be wet, to avoid long-term soil moisture.

Temperature & Light

Good conditions should be kept in the sun for about 6 hours/day, if you leave it in the office, water a little, but the plants will be difficult to flower so keep them in the office. Suitable temperature from 15°C - 28°C
