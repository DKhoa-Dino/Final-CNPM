How to take care

Depending on environmental conditions, we will have different ways to care for plants. Happy trees can be planted indoors, but need a different care regimen from plants grown outside

Water

Plants are of moderate hydrophilic type, less water, the leaves will be yellow, crunchy, more water, the leaves will droop and turn black. Therefore, it is important to keep the soil moist at all times. If planting in a pot, it is necessary to add a layer of gravel underneath it to open the pot because plants do not like being submerged in water.

Light

Happiness trees flower when planted in a well-lit garden or in large spaces. Plants usually flower in the middle of spring, with white or pale yellow flowers, popular and fragrant. When grown in an office, less light than plants very rarely flower. If the tree of Happiness has flowers in this case, it means your family is about to have good news or good luck.
