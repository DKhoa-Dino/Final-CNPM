How to take care

Podocarpus Macrophyllus is very easy to care for and can be adapted to all conditions, if you plant it in a large garden, you may not need to take care of anything but the tree still lives and grows. As for plants grown and used as ornamental plants in the office, it is necessary to occasionally let the plants out in the sun, and water once a week.

Water

Podocarpus Macrophyllus is also considered an office bonsai because it is quite water-loving and easy to live, you should water once a week under normal conditions. When the weather is highly humid, or hot, it can increase or decrease. Although plants like moisture, but should not always be so moist, it will create good conditions for fungi and how harmful organisms to grow.

Temperature

The best temperature for Podocarpus Macrophyllus is between 20ºC - 30ºC. The tree is difficult to live and is likely to die when the climate is below 0ºC and when hot above 40ºC it is susceptible to a fungus called Seiridium Cardinale. When infected with this fungus, the plant will dry and black bud and gradually die. This species thrives when it has a stable root system. If you are planting a garden, you may not need watering or fertilizer, just pruning is enough.
