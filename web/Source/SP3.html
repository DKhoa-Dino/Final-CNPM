How to take care

Goldcrest Tree can be grown both indoors and outdoors. It is easy to care for and less pestilent.

Water

Plants have the same water requirements as other office plants. Water 2 times a week, each time watering about 1/2 to 3/4 of soil, depending on the size of the pot. The larger the pot, the longer it will hold water and vice versa, depending on the humidity of the environment, balance the water so that the soil is not too wet or too dry, if you leave the plant outside every day you can also water.

Light

Plants can survive in low light conditions, in environments with only electricity fluorescence, but for the plants to be beautiful and bright, you should dry them for 30 minutes to 1 hour per day. Or when you go home, bring the tree to a place with light. When you do, you can bring it back in. The appropriate temperature is from 21 - 26 degrees C. However, avoid heat shock, for example, in a cold air-conditioned room, the temperature outside and inside is up to 10 degrees C apart, you bring the tree out, so it's easy to do. the tree died in heat shock
