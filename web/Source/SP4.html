How to take care

Ivy is a lush green tree all year round, with strong vitality that can adapt to cold weather but cannot withstand hot weather in high temperature environments. Plants are easy to care for, easy to live in. So it is very suitable for office plants, and indoor plants

Water

As a plant that prefers shady and humid places, plants need regular water supply, you can water them twice a week if kept indoors, and can be watered daily if left outside in weather. hot dry summer. Plants should be watered early in the morning and preferably in the cool afternoon. But be careful not to let the soil stay moist for a long time, it will easily rot plants and create conditions for mold.

Light

The most suitable temperature for an evergreen tree to grow is from 15-25 degrees, plants need light to photosynthesize and grow can be fluorescent light, but it is best in the early morning and early morning. avoid strong summer noon sunlight, easily lead to wilting of yellow leaves.
