How to take care

Belonging to an office tree, Lucky Tree is easy to take care of and can live well in an office with air conditioning and little light. Should occasionally expose the plant to the sun to kill bacteria in the soil and also help plants stay beautiful.

Water

It is advisable to leave Lucky Tree in a cool place with light sunlight, do not leave it outside under the summer noon sun without shading. Because the plants are kept in cool conditions such as office conditions, indoors, just provide a little water for the plants about 1 week, water once every time enough moisture, or 1 week watering 2 times per time of moisture 1/2 soil, should not be watered much, leaving plants in the rain could easily water the grass layer and lead to plant damage.

Temperature

The right temperature for this to live and the leaves are always fresh from 15 - 28 degrees. At temperatures above 35 degrees, plants are susceptible to withering and burning leaves.
