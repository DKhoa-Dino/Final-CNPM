Plant tree

Born during the time of President Ho Chi Minh, he attaches great importance to the planting of trees for afforestation. Over the past 60 years, the Tree planting Festival initiated by him has become a powerful, extensive and practical mass movement. Continuing that spirit, for many years, the Party and State have always paid attention to the planting of trees to cause forests in association with environmental and resource protection. However, economic activity and growth have put pressure on social life as population growth and urbanization are taking place increasingly strongly and broadly. Green belts that protect the environment are not interested in protection planning, especially in young urban areas. MEANING AND ORIGINAL SOURCE EVENTS

Launched in 2017 in Sydney (Australia), Earth Hour is the only, largest and most engaged event on Earth so far. Small actions such as turning off electrical appliances when not in use, saving water, planting a row of trees will bring great meaning to the community, forming an energy-saving habit, protecting the environment in all year. When was Earth Hour born? Starting in 2007 in Sydney (Australia), the number of participants was only 2 million people. Earth Hour is an annual international event, run by the World Wildlife Fund (WWF), which urges households and businesses to turn off their lights and electrical appliances. Big people come to live for one hour at 20:30 to 21:30 p.m. (local time) on the last Saturday of March every year.

Why remove plastic straws?

Restaurants, shops, supermarkets, .. paper straws are being greeted with great enthusiasm. Instead of using plastic straws as before, everyone has turned to paper straws. In 2020, Starbucks officially removes plastic straws from its headquarters. Seattle - a beautiful US city has also imposed a ban on plastic straws since July 2018. In many other countries such as the UK and Ireland, they are also testing and eliminating plastic straws. One study showed that, in the United States, Americans emit 500 million plastic cigarettes every day. All of them only used a straw once and thrown away. Most of these straws are sent to the oceans, polluting beaches around the world.

The importance of paper bags to protect the environment

In this modern life, especially in Vietnam, it can be said that plastic bags are widely used, used in unlimited quantities, flooded in markets, supermarkets or shops. Wherever there is trading activity, plastic bags appear. Most plastic bags are used only a few times and then dumped in landfills, streets, rivers or underground. These plastic bags, even in the environment, can not decompose for thousands of years, causing many dangerous consequences to the health and environment of humans and organisms. Therefore, every person, every business needs to be conscious of environmental protection and use of environmentally friendly products. In particular, the birth of environmentally friendly paper bags using safe recycled materials is supported and trusted by many people.
